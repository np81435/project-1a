My Favorite New Orleans Restaurants

The city of New Orleans is well know for its food culture. While outsiders only think of the French Quarter and gumbo, we locals know that this city has a diverse ethnic food culture that spans Orleans, Jefferson and St. Bernard Parishes.

1 Drago's, Metarie LA

Drago's Restaurant has been a culinary staple ever since Drago and Klara Cvitanovich opened their restaurant in 1969. They set out to offer delicious seafood and a good time to guests for a reasonable price. The must order item here is their famous chargrilled oysters!

2 Nine Roses, Gretna LA

Nine Roses has the biggest menu of any restaurant in New Orleans, including every Vietnamese dish you’ve had anywhere else, plus many more you’ve never seen locally–except, perhaps, in a Vietnamese home. The dish to order is the Clay Pot Catfish!

3 Rocky and Carlo's, Chalmette LA

This iconic restaurant is known far and wide for its fabulously oversized portions of Sicilian dishes and New Orleans classics. The dish to order here is veal parmasean with their famous mac'n cheese!

4 Pizza delicious New orleans, la

Is there good pizza in New Orleans you ask? The answer is heck yes! Located in the Bywater, Pizza Deliciuos is one of our favorite resaurants. Don't let the name fool you, they make more than pizza. The dish to get here is house made pasta. This is the best pasta I have ever had!
